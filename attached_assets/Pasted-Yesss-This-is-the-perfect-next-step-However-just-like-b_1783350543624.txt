Bro, something else just crossed my mind that would make your mum's business much safer.

Right now, the app keeps a record of **payments** and **extra expenditures**, but it doesn't explain **why** money changed. I think every financial action should also have a **reason code** (e.g., "Engine Repair", "Tyre Replacement", "Accident Damage", "Fuel Advance") and an optional note. If, three years from now, your mum asks, *"Why did we spend ₦185,000 on this driver?"* the system should answer immediately instead of just showing a number.

That's the kind of feature people usually only think about after they've been using a system for a while. Since we're building from scratch, we can get it right the first time.
